We would like to take a cruise up Lake George instead of renting a boat. Are their any large tour boats which we can take a cruise up the Lake on?

The Lake George Steamboat Co. in Lake George Village (45 minutes away) offers a variety of cruises on different boats. They can be contacted at: 800-553-2628.

We love to fish. What should we know about fishing on Lake George?

Lake George has some of the greatest sport fishing in the Northeast. Land-locked salmon, small mouth bass, lake trout and rainbow trout are all one cast away! Bait shops are conveniently located about 20 minutes away in Whitehall. You will need to purchase a fishing license though from NY State. This is strictly enforced.

We want to go hiking. Can you recommend a really good trail?

Black Mountain is the tallest mountain on Lake George. There are two trails up the mountain maintained by New York State. One starts from the lake side (only accessible by boat) the other begins from the land side. The hike takes about an hour each way but the view from the top is unbelievable! You won't be disappointed.

On a rainy day if our family wants to go to a mall or see a movie where are the closest malls located?

The closest malls and cinemas are: a) Aviation Mall in Glens Falls (45 minutes away) or b) the new Rutland Mall in Rutland, Vermont (45 minutes away).

Are there any log cabins in the Lake George are that are open in winter?

You should have plenty of options, including the log cabins at Trout House Village Resort, 30 miles from Lake George on the lake’s western shore.

Where can one go for a horse back ride in Lake George?

If you’re still looking for a place to stay, mosey on up to the Roaring Brook Ranch Resort & Conference Center, which has stables and various riding programs for its guests ($28 per person per ride). Nearby, Bennett’s Riding Stable (518.696.4444) is open to the public and offers various horseback-riding excursions. Or, you could book yourself for a stay on a dude ranch like 1000 Acres Dude Ranch Resort), tucked away less than 15 miles from the southern end of Lake George in Stony Creek.

Are there any places to take a 13 year old boy to hit golf balls at a driving range in or near Lake George and are there any batting cages in Lake George?

You can find batting cages at Lake George Batting Cages, located at Route 9 and Prospect Mountain Highway (518.668.2531). There are a few driving ranges, including Queensbury Country Club (518.793.3711) and the more high-tech -- and expensive -- Evergreens Indoor Golf Center (518.793.6699).

How far is Great Escape from Lake George and water slides?

About 15 minutes from Lake George Village. If you don’t go back onto the Northway you will go straight down Canada Street and continue past the outlets and then its just about a mile or so from there.
